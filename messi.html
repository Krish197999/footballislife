About Lionel Messi

Lionel Messi, often regarded as one of the greatest football players of all time, is an Argentine forward known for his exceptional dribbling skills and goal-scoring prowess.

He spent the majority of his career with FC Barcelona before joining Paris Saint-Germain (PSG).

Messi has won numerous Ballon d'Or awards and holds records for the most goals in a calendar year.

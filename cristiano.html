About Cristiano Ronaldo

Cristiano Ronaldo is one of the greatest footballers of all time. He has won numerous awards and accolades throughout his career, including multiple FIFA Ballon d'Or awards.

He has played for several top clubs, including Sporting CP, Manchester United, Real Madrid, Juventus, and most recently, he returned to Manchester United.

Ronaldo has scored over 850 goals for his club teams and is also the all-time leading scorer for the Portuguese national team.

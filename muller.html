About Gerd Muller

Gerd Müller, known as "Der Bomber," was a legendary German footballer celebrated for his exceptional goal-scoring prowess.

He played the majority of his career at Bayern Munich and was renowned for his remarkable finishing ability, netting 68 goals in 62 appearances for the German national team.

Müller's incredible goal-scoring record stood out, earning him admiration as one of the greatest strikers in football history.
